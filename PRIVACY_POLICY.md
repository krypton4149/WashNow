# Privacy Policy for KwikWash

**Last updated:** February 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how **KwikWash** ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

By using KwikWash, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly and information that is collected automatically when you use the app.

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account registration (customers):** Name, email address, phone number, vehicle make and model, vehicle registration number, and password.
- **Account registration (service center owners):** Email, password, and after sign-up: business details including service center name, address, phone, location (latitude/longitude), opening hours, and week-off days.
- **Profile:** When you edit your profile, we may store your name, phone number, and (for owners) your service center address and location.
- **Bookings:** When you book a car wash, we collect and store the selected service center, date, time, vehicle number, optional notes, and (when applicable) the service chosen.
- **Support and communications:** Any information you send to us when contacting support or feedback.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device and app usage:** We may receive a device token (e.g. Firebase Cloud Messaging token) to send you push notifications about bookings, reminders, and updates. We may also collect your device type and operating system (e.g. Android) for compatibility and support.
- **Location:**  
  - **Customers:** With your permission, we use your device’s location to find nearby car wash centers and show you relevant results. Location may be sent to our servers to fetch nearby centers.  
  - **Owners:** With your permission, we use your device’s location to set or update your service center’s address and coordinates.
- **Local storage:** The app stores data on your device (e.g. login token, user profile, theme preference, and cached data such as bookings and alerts) to keep you signed in and to improve performance.

### 1.3 Payment Information

Payments are processed by third-party providers (e.g. Stripe, PayPal). We do not store your full card number or full payment credentials. We may receive and store payment-related identifiers (e.g. transaction or booking IDs) to link payments to your bookings. Please refer to the respective payment provider’s privacy policy for how they handle your payment data.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account (customer or owner).
- Process and manage your car wash bookings and send related notifications.
- Show you nearby service centers (using location when you allow it).
- Enable owners to manage their service center profile and address (including location when you allow it).
- Send push notifications (e.g. booking confirmations, reminders, status updates) using your device token.
- Process payments through our payment partners and link payments to your bookings.
- Improve the app, fix errors, and personalize your experience.
- Respond to your support requests and communicate with you about the app.
- Comply with applicable laws and enforce our terms.

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

- **Service providers:** With vendors who help us run the app (e.g. hosting, analytics, push notifications, payment processors). These providers are required to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.
- **Legal requirements:** When required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
- **Business transfers:** In connection with a merger, sale of assets, or acquisition, in which case your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on secure servers and, where applicable, in encrypted form. We use industry-standard measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
- Sensitive data such as passwords are stored in an encrypted form. We do not store full payment card details; payments are handled by certified payment processors.
- Data stored on your device (e.g. via the app’s local storage) is subject to your device’s security. We recommend keeping your device locked and your OS updated.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- We retain your account and profile data for as long as your account is active. We may retain certain data after account closure where required for legal, regulatory, or legitimate business purposes (e.g. completed transactions, dispute resolution).
- Cached data on your device (e.g. bookings, alerts) may be cleared when you clear app data or uninstall the app. You can also log out to limit what remains in local storage.

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and operational requirements.
- **Opt-out of marketing:** Unsubscribe from promotional communications (we use push mainly for transactional messages such as booking updates).
- **Location:** You can deny or revoke location permission in your device settings. The app may still work with reduced functionality (e.g. you may need to search for centers manually).
- **Push notifications:** You can disable push notifications in your device settings for the app.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details in the "Contact Us" section below. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.
